## Rules

- Record decisions that change scope, appetite, selected direction, or source-of-truth artifacts.
- Link decisions to affected requirement, shape, place, affordance, store, or slice IDs when possible.
- Do not use the decision log for every small implementation detail.
- When implementation reality creates drift, record the human decision about whether code or plan changes.
